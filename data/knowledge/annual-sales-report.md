# Annual Sales Report 2025

## Executive Summary

The 2025 plan prioritized repeatable subscription revenue across finance platform, analytics, planning, operations, services, and mobile products. The sales plan assumes steady expansion across North, South, East, and West regions.

## Budget Context

The 2026 annual sales target is documented in the current budget document. Historical budget targets increase each year to support a transparent five-year forecast demonstration.

## Operating Focus

Fourth-quarter demand is expected to be stronger than the first quarter because customers commonly finalize annual planning and reporting budgets late in the calendar year.
