# Market Risks

## Key Risks

Discount pressure from lower-priced competitors may reduce net revenue or gross margin. Longer customer approval cycles can defer planned purchases, especially outside the fourth-quarter budget cycle.

## Mitigations

Maintain approved discount thresholds, prioritize high-margin finance-platform offerings, and review regional pipeline quality before committing to optimistic scenarios.
