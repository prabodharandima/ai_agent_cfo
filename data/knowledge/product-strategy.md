# Product Strategy

## Growth Priorities

Prioritize Ledger Pro and Insight Analytics for enterprise expansion. Pair Growth Planner with finance-platform renewals to increase planning adoption without relying on unapproved discounting.

## Portfolio Approach

Beacon Support and Horizon Mobile provide broader entry points, while Atlas Operations supports operational buyer needs. Review product mix monthly to protect gross profit as regional demand changes.
