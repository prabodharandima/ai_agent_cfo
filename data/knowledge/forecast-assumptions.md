# Forecast Assumptions

## Planning Assumptions

The MVP forecast uses historical annual net sales as its baseline. Expected results assume continued product availability, stable unit economics, and no acquisition or major divestiture.

## Scenarios

The conservative scenario reflects slower conversion and discount pressure. The expected scenario follows the historical trend. The optimistic scenario assumes stronger finance-platform and analytics demand with controlled discounting.

## Limitation

This forecast is a planning demonstration and is not professional financial advice.
