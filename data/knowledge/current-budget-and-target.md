# Current Budget And Annual Target

## Annual Target

The FY2026 sales target is $3,000,000. The FY2026 gross-profit target is $960,000, which represents a planned 32 percent gross-profit margin.

## Monthly Targets

Monthly targets are seasonally weighted. January begins below the annual monthly average, while October through December carry progressively higher targets to reflect year-end planning demand.

## Budget Assumptions

The target assumes active products remain available in all four sales regions, pricing remains stable, and discounts stay within approved commercial terms.
